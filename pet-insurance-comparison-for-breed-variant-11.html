Pet Insurance Glossary

  • Accident: a sudden injury or accident that requires medical attention
  • Illness: a condition that affects a pet's health, such as a disease or infection
  • Pre-existing condition: a condition that existed before purchasing pet insurance
  • Deductible: the amount paid by the policyholder before insurance coverage kicks in
  • Co-pay: a percentage of the medical bill paid by the policyholder
