Pre-existing conditions

Pre-existing conditions are health issues that existed before the policy took effect. Pet insurance often excludes pre-existing conditions and will not cover them unless you take out a policy that includes a waiting period and provides coverage for conditions diagnosed after that period.

Breed-specific risks

Breed Variant 93 may be more prone to certain health issues that could be exacerbated by genetic factors. Pet owners must assess the likelihood of these risks occurring and how to mitigate them.
