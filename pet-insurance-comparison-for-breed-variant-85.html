Key Considerations

  • Breed Variant 85 tends to be susceptible to joint and mobility issues; ensure the chosen plan covers orthopedic-related expenses.
  • Prioritise a plan offering up-to-date genetic testing for inherited conditions.
  • Breed Variant 85 is generally a long-lived breed; consider the provider's coverage up to the age of 18.
  • Pre-existing conditions are generally excluded; opt for a provider with flexible renewal policies.
  • An online claim submission process can significantly streamline the claims process.
